INTRODUCTION

MISCONCEPTIONS

NFT is not just jpeg pictures. Many people nowadays sell their jpeg pictures plus a discord community and call it “NFTs”. This is misleading.

NFT is the technology that proves digital ownership. Don’t let the speculators mislead you.

WHAT ARE NON­FUNGIBLE TOKENS (NFT)?

NFTs are like the receipts or certifications for digital items. When an item has an NFT, also commonly referred to as “became an NFT” or “mint the NFT”, it became a unique item. No one can copy/paste it.
